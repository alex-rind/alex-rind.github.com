  <h2 id="student">supervised student projects</h2>

  <dl>
    <dt>05/2011 &ndash; 04/2012</dt>
    <dd>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/">
        <img src="img/TimeRider3_Teaser_112.png" alt="TimeRider" width="112" height="82" align="right" />
      </a>
      <p class="project">A Comparison of Static and Dynamic Visualizations for Time-Oriented Data (M.Sc. thesis)</p>
      <div>Animated scatter plot and two variants of small multiples were
      experimentally compared in an extended version of the TimeRider prototype.</div>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/" class="external">project webpage</a>
    </dd>

    <dt>01/2011 &ndash; 11/2011</dt>
    <dd>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/semzoom/">
        <img src="img/stz-eval-h22-boxplot.png" alt="Semantic Zoom" width="111" height="113" align="right" />
      </a>
       <p class="project">Empirical Evaluation of a Visualization Technique with Semantic Zoom (M.Sc. thesis)</p>
      <div>The effectiveness of two visualization techniques for time series
      data in combination with qualitative abstractions was compared in a
      controlled experiment.</div>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/semzoom/" class="external">project webpage</a>
    </dd>

    <dt>02/2010 &ndash; 10/2010</dt>
    <dd>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/">
        <img src="img/TimeRider2_Teaser_112.png" alt="TimeRider" width="112" height="84" align="right" />
      </a>
      <p class="project">TimeRider&mdash;Visualizing Patient Cohorts by Animated Scatter Plots (ctd.)</p>
      <div>The TimeRider prototype is extended with features to synchronize
      patient histories, select patients, save settings, further usability
      improvements.</div>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/" class="external">project webpage</a>
    </dd>

    <dt>03/2009 &ndash; 11/2009</dt>
    <dd>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/semzoom/">
        <img src="img/SemZoom_Teaser_112.gif" alt="Semantic Zoom" width="112" height="86" align="right" />
      </a>
       <p class="project">Semantic Zoom of Time-oriented Quantitative Data</p>
      <div>A reusable software component for the qualitatively enriched
      and space-efficient visualization of time-oriented data based on the
      prefuse toolkit.</div>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/semzoom/" class="external">project webpage</a>
    </dd>

    <dt>01/2009 &ndash; 07/2009</dt>
    <dd>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/">
        <img src="img/TimeRider1_Teaser_112.gif" alt="TimeRider" width="112" height="97" align="right" />
      </a>
      <p class="project">TimeRider&mdash;Visualizing Patient Cohorts by Animated Scatter Plots</p>
      <div>A prototypical visualization system allows medical analysts to
      explore the dynamics of patient cohorts. It provides a scatter plot to
      view the relationship between two variables and animation to understand
      development over time. Further it tackles irregular sampling and data
      wear, which arise from the medical scenario.</div>
      <a href="http://ieg.ifs.tuwien.ac.at/projects/timerider/" class="external">project webpage</a>
    </dd>
  </dl>
